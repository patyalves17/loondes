About HILT

How I Look Today

or HILT , is our academic project in the MBA for mobile development with IONIC, Angular and Cordova. It is offered as is and there will be no guarantees or upgrades.

HILT is a place to archive and easily retrive that information you never remember about your looks. What blade os scissor the hair stylist used, what color code and the name of your hair cut.

With HILT you never forget why you looked so pretty that day. ;-)
